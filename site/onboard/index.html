Bring your game

Igroteka never uploads or hosts game files. Your copy of C&C Generals: Zero Hour is imported into this browser's private storage, on this device only.

1 · From your Steam install

Pick the game folder — usually Steam/steamapps/common/Command and Conquer Generals Zero Hour (macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/Steam/steamapps/common/…)

2 · Or from an asset pack (.zip)

Coming from another device? Zip the game folder on your computer (right-click → Compress), AirDrop it here, then pick the zip.

3 · From your cloud

iCloud Drive works through the pickers above (Files app). Direct Google Drive / Dropbox import is on the roadmap.

Ready ✓

Everything Zero Hour needs is imported.

Don't own it? Zero Hour is on Steam (C&C Ultimate Collection). Files stay in your browser's private storage (OPFS) and can be cleared any time from site settings.
